Athletics At St. Andrew's

"St. Andrew's offers 10 sports for girls and 11 sports for boys. All sports field varsity and junior varsity teams, and some a third level. Most students begin their careers on lower-level teams and work their way up to varsity. Students are required to play two sports during their III Form (freshman) and IV Form (sophomore) years, and one sport during their V Form (junior) and VI Form (senior) years. St. Andrew's competes in the Delaware Independent Schools Conference, which is comprised of six independent schools located not more than 45 minutes from our campus. We have won nearly 40 state championships in our nearly 90-year history as a School, and some of our teams have received national and international recognition." Some examples seen below:
